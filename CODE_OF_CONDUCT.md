# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We welcome everyone to contribute to pdf_pilot. We are committed to making participation a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

**Encouraged:**
- Be respectful and constructive
- Help newcomers learn
- Focus on ideas, not individuals
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully

**Unacceptable:**
- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Enforcement

Report issues to the project maintainer. All complaints will be reviewed and addressed fairly.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces (issues, PRs, discussions) and when representing the project publicly.
